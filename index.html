About Me
My Road to Design and Build Websites

My name is Brayan Torres and I am bilingual in Spanish and English. My passion for design and creating web pages started when I was given the opportunity to have technology in my hands by a Electronic device 12 years ago.

Watching videos of Web development, motivates me to do better on my plans. I'm driven to accomplish many goals to come in the future. I've always been that type of person who believes can do more.

My first job when I started was at a Almond Company in the Fresno Valley, where I worked seasonal. I also worked for a Irrigation Company, which helped me to go back to school for web development.

Then, I decide to follow my dreams and I attended Fresno Pacific BootCamp to take a career in the field that fascinates me the most, "web development". I always follow this quote "Nothing lasts forever, so do what you have in your mind ", which motivates me.

Now I am more than ready to take you to the web world!
